Name Variations  
For the real name "Kaimachnikov Mikhail Konstantinovich," the following variations may apply:  
- Different languages: Russian and English  
- Uppercase letters: All first letters of each word in the name may be capitalized  
- Different transliteration methods for specific sounds:  
  - х = kh = h  
  - й = i = y  
- Various word and initial separators:  
  - No spaces, spaces, underscores  
- Initials may be used  

Pseudonym Guidelines  
For pseudonyms, the first letters of acronyms can be in either uppercase or lowercase, while all other letters must be in lowercase.  

Role Name Guidelines  
For role names, the following checks should be performed:  
- Minimum of two languages: English and Russian, focusing on word translations  
- Uppercase letters may include:  
  - Any letter starting the object name  
  - First letters of key words (excluding articles, conjunctions, prepositions) or abbreviations in the name  
- The same rules for capitalization apply as for pseudonyms, with the addition that real names may also follow these guidelines.
